How it works

Three steps between you and a genuinely informed choice at the shelf.

1

Scan

Point your camera at any barcode. Underneath pulls the full ingredient list in seconds, and reads the label directly with your camera when a product is missing from the database.

2

Score

Every product gets one clear score from 0 to 100, weighted toward how processed it is. Additives carry the most weight, then the processing level, then the nutrition.

3

Swap

See cleaner alternatives in the same category, ranked by how they actually score, so you can upgrade the things you already buy every week.

What makes Underneath different

Most scanners lead with calories and sugar. We lead with processing, because that is where the evidence keeps pointing.

Processing first scoring

A food is judged first on how far it sits from real, whole ingredients, not just its macros.

Additive detection

We flag the emulsifiers, sweeteners, colours and preservatives that hide behind vague label wording.

NOVA classification

Every product is mapped to the NOVA processing scale used by nutrition researchers worldwide.

Honest, never fear based

We do not scaremonger. A benign ingredient is scored as benign. The aim is clarity, not panic.

Reads any label

No barcode match? Point your camera at the ingredient list and Underneath reads and scores it for you.

Cleaner swaps

Real alternatives in the same aisle, ranked by how they genuinely score, not by who is advertising.

The food changed. The labels did not keep up.

Everyday staples that used to be a handful of ingredients are now built from additives, refined starches and industrial processing. The packaging still looks wholesome.

Underneath reads what is actually in the modern version and tells you, in one honest number, how far it has drifted from real food, so the choice is yours to make with the full picture.

About Underneath

Underneath is built by a small independent team who were tired of reading the front of a package one way and the ingredient list another. We think the true processing level of everyday food should be readable in seconds by anyone, standing in any aisle.

Our scoring is grounded in the NOVA processing classification and published research on additives and ultra-processed food. We do not sell your data and we do not take money to move a product up a score.
